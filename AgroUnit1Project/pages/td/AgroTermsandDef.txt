Native
Plants evolved in the region and for that region
Non-Native
Plants have been introduced (by accident or on purpose) to the region
Weed
A Plant living in a place that it is not wanted
Invasive Plant
Non-native plants that cause economic, environmental, or human harm
Noxious
A legal distinction by a natural resource or agricultural authority for the purpose of regulation
Naturalized
Plants are non native and can maintain a population without human aid
Annual Plants
Plants complete their life cycle in one year
Perennial Plants
Plants live for more than two years and regenerate each year from protected buds
Biennial Plants
Plants complete their life cycle in two years.
